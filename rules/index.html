Welcome

Welcome to the Taiwan Returnee Club (TRC) Discord server, we are thrilled to have you here!


For starters, please read the rules so that we are all on the same page on how to act nicely and make the community a positive and vibrant one. We are NOT a political server, thus, if you want to talk politics, send them in the #辯論┊debate channel. Likewise, deliberate provocation causing server conflicts will cause you to be muted or even banned. Thank you!



Server Rules

  1. Any form of profanity, bullying, obscenity (or NSFW), hatred, racism or discrimination is strictly prohibited and will not be tolerated. This also includes aggressionism or use of insensitive remarks.
  2. Respect and follow the instructions of staff members whenever possible.
  3. Respect fellow members’ personal decisions and privacy where any form of harassment or excessive pinging will result in a mute or direct ban from the server.
  4. Having an appropriate account name, profile picture or background images is a must.
  5. Identity fraud or impersonating other people are strictly prohibited, likewise, we do not allow use of "alt accounts".
  6. Exploitation of staff members, rules, and bots will result in an immediate ban. Likewise, hacking or tampering with the server and its systems are also prohibited.
  7. This is not a dating server whereby public displays of affection or flirtatious behaviour should be kept minimally.
  8. We do not allow any form of advertising or spamming of invite links to TRC, OR to other Discord servers.
  9. Respect Discord’s rules and applicable Terms of Service (ToS) statements.


How we Moderate

This is our general process in handling members who break server rules. Please note that verbal reminder, warning, and notice are synonymous in this server. They mean the same thing. A verbal reminder is ANY reminder given to you verbally by the staff which may be in the server or in DMs (direct messages).


  1. @First Strike, @Second Strike and @Last Strike: nothing happens, you just get pinged and verbally reminded.
  2. @First Warning: your account will be muted for 1 day.
  3. @Second Warning: your account will be muted for 2 days, you cannot change nicknames for 14 days, and 14 days ban from entering any voice chats (VCs) in the server.
  4. @Last Warning: you are muted for 7 days, will not able to change nicknames, and are banned from all voice chats in the server.
  5. Finally, we ban members who violates our rules again after @Last Warning.

Notice how we all start out as green in the @Norm after verification. This means you have no infractions recorded in this server. As you get closer to getting banned, your color changes to yellow and then shifts to dangerous red right before your ban.


Respect all staff decisions. Calmly discuss with a staff member if you think there's been a mistake. If you throw a tantrum, your strike will quickly progress to a ban. Please do not throw a tantrum as all bans are final.

伺服器規則

  1. 任何形式的褻瀆、欺凌、淫穢（或 NSFW）、仇恨、種族歧視都是嚴格禁止的，並且不會被容忍。這還包括攻擊性或使用麻木不仁的言論。
  2. 請盡量尊重並遵循工作人員的指示。
  3. 請尊重其他成員的個人決定和隱私，任何形式的騷擾或過度Ping會導致你的賬號被靜音或從群組中移除。
  4. 您必須使用合適的賬號名稱、個人頭像（PFP）或背景圖片在此伺服器內。
  5. 嚴禁身份欺詐或冒充他人，同樣，我們也不允許使用 “替代賬號” 或「alt accounts」。
  6. 剝削TRC工作人員、規則或者機器人將導致您的賬號從群組中立即移除並黑名單化。同樣，我們也強烈禁止對此伺服器及其他相關係統做出黑客攻擊或篡改的行為。
  7. 此伺服器不是交友平台並請盡量減少公開示愛或調情的行為。
  8. 我們不允許向TRC或其他Discord群組發送任何形式的廣告、邀請鏈接或垃圾郵件。
  9. 請尊重Discord的規則和服務條款（ToS）聲明。


我們的管理模式

這是我們處理違反伺服器規則的成員的一般流程。請注意，口頭提醒、口頭警告和口頭通知是同義詞（在本伺服器中）。他們的意思是一樣的。口頭提醒是由可能在伺服器或私訊中的工作人員給您的任何口頭提醒。


  1. @First Strike, @Second Strike 跟 @Last Strike：什麼事都沒有發生，你只是被Ping和口頭提醒。
  2. @First Warning：你的賬號將被靜音一天。
  3. @Second Warning：你的賬號將被靜音兩天，禁止14內天更改暱稱、加上禁止14天進入本服務器的語音聊天室（VC）。
  4. @Last Warning：你的賬號將被靜音7天，永久限制更改暱稱、加上禁止進入本服務器的語音聊天室。
  5. 最後，已經有 @Last Warning 的成員再次出錯，我們就會直接把你列入黑名單。

請注意，在 @Norm 中，我們都是從綠色開始的。@Norm 表示您在此服務器中沒有記錄任何違規行為。當您接近被禁止時，您的顏色會變為黃色，然後在您被禁止之前變為危險的紅色。


請尊重TRC工作人員的決定。如果您認為有錯誤，請冷靜地與工作人員討論。如果您發脾氣，您的罷工將很快進入禁令，或者如果您已被禁止並發脾氣，則沒有任何作用，因為所有禁令都是最終的。謝謝！
